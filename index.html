Our History

The Muñoz Legacy

Muñoz and Photography. A name and a profession. Both together as one in a family tradition of excellence and service. The Muñoz name has stood as one of the longest lasting photographic traditions with its roots stemming from Cuba. With 100 years of experience and professionalism, Enrique Muñoz Studio finds its self turning the page on a new century of photography.

Enrique Muñoz Studio is located in Southwest Miami-Dade, where it has been in business for over 50 years. With the 3rd and 4th generations currently operating the business, the studio is at the forefront of photography. Providing the highest quality of products in the industry, Enrique Muñoz Studio will continue to honor its clientele with its tradition of excellence and service. While longevity in today's society is something to marvel at, the changes in the photography business during these past decades have not left us behind.

We embraced digital photography in it's infancy and thus were able to ride the wave of technology in to today's fast-paced market. From communions to graduations, quinceaneras to weddings, Enrique Muñoz Studio has the service, quality, products and personnel to make your special moments in life a memory to cherish.
